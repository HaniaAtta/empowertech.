ABOUT US




Empower Tech Women is a passionate community dedicated to leveling the playing field for underprivileged women in the ever-evolving world of technology. The future of tech is brighter when it reflects the richness of diverse voices and perspectives. Our mission is to empower women from all backgrounds by connecting them with scholarship opportunities for in-demand tech courses. Financial barriers can often be the biggest hurdle in pursuing a tech education. Here at Empower Tech Women, we want to break down those barriers and make tech education accessible to all deserving women.
Scholarships for Your Tech Dreams:
We curate a comprehensive database of scholarships specifically designed for women in technology. Our scholarships cover a wide range of in-demand tech fields, from coding and cybersecurity to data science and artificial intelligence. No matter your area of interest, we're here to help you find the financial support you need to launch your tech career.
More Than Scholarships - A Supportive Community:
Empower Tech Women is more than just scholarships. We are a vibrant community that offers support, encouragement, and mentorship. Connect with like-minded women on our platform, share experiences, and learn from each other. We believe that by building a strong network of support, we can all rise together and achieve our goals.
Join Us and Take Flight: We invite you to join our thriving community of empowered women in tech. Explore our scholarship database, connect with other members, and take charge of your future. Together, let's unlock your potential and watch your tech dreams take flight!
